The **Geometry, Robotics, and Dynamical Systems (GRaDS) Lab** is a robotics and
control research group in the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering at the
**New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT)**, led by
**[Dr. Adeel Akhtar](https://adeelakhtar.com/)**. The lab was established in early 2025.

We study the **geometric, nonlinear, and hybrid control** foundations of intelligent
robots, with applications spanning mobile and aerial robotics, drones, and
safety-critical autonomous systems. Our work brings together differential geometry,
nonlinear and hybrid systems theory, optimization, and stochastic dynamics to build
robots that operate safely, reliably, and efficiently in the real world.
